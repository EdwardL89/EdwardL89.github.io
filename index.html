Summary

Hello! My name is Edward Liu, I am a full-time software engineer at WillowTree Apps, and I graduated from Virginia Tech with a BS in computer science in 2021. In the past, I helped develop a military reconnaissance software application that provides Army Engineer units faster, safer, and more accurate execution of digital land mapping as part of an internship at Reinventing Geospatial, Inc. where I was a software engineering intern. I've also applied my skills during an internship the following Summer at Fannie Mae, developing an application that modernized my department's database job completion system, helping to significantly increase the productivity of my team.

During the 2018-2019 academic year, I was a Research Assistant at Virginia Tech, collaborating with a professor to develop an Android app that identifies a plant pathogen from photos taken by users, with the capability for users to send those photos through a private server from their phones to a computer on campus to be further analyzed by my professor. And, while I'm giving back to my fellow Hokies through my work, I'm also earning high academic achievements such as being on the Dean's list and being recognized as a Pamplin Leader through Virginia Tech's Pamplin Scholars Program. In my future, I plan on continuing to fuel my passion for software development by continuously learning, exploring all the fields computer science has to offer, and providing value to the professional world.
